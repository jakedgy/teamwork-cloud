Wait for the nginx controller's external address, then open:

  http://<external-address>/webapp

The Artemis credentials are intentionally required and immutable at install
time. Keep the password out of values files and supply it with a secret values
source. To change credentials in this prototype, uninstall and reinstall it.

PVC sizes are install-time settings. Set storageClass.create=false and provide
storageClass.name to use an existing StorageClass instead of chart-managed
auto-ebs. StatefulSet PVCs are deleted with their prototype workloads.

Images use pinned version tags. Digest validation and pinning are deliberately
deferred until Task 9, after validation on EKS.
